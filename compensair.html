What is Compensair?

Compensair is a flight compensation service that helps passengers claim money they are legally owed under EU Regulation 261/2004. If your flight was delayed by 3+ hours, cancelled, or overbooked, you may be entitled to up to €600 per passenger — and Compensair handles the entire claims process on your behalf.

The service is completely free to check, and Compensair only charges a commission if your claim is successful — meaning there is zero financial risk to trying.

Key Features

  • ✓ Up to €600 per passenger under EU261
  • ✓ No win, no fee — zero upfront cost
  • ✓ Covers flights up to 3 years ago
  • ✓ Free eligibility check — takes 2 minutes
  • ✓ Handles airline negotiations for you
  • ✓ Legal action taken if airline refuses

Pricing & Plans

Compensair is completely free to use. If your claim is successful, Compensair takes a commission of around 25–35% of the compensation amount. You keep the rest. There are no upfront fees, no hidden charges, and no cost at all if your claim is not successful.
