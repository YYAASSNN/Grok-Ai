Looking for the cheapest flights in 2026? Aviasales is one of the most powerful flight search engines available — especially strong for Europe, Asia, and international routes. It often finds cheaper combinations than Google Flights or Skyscanner by surfacing hidden routes and flexible options.

Why Aviasales is Great for Cheap Flights

  • Compares 2,000+ airlines and ticket offices instantly
  • "Everywhere" search mode — discover the cheapest destinations from your city
  • Flexible dates calendar and automatic price alerts
  • Shows direct flights, smart layovers, and hidden gems
  • Clean interface with clear price breakdowns and no hidden fees

How to Use Aviasales Like a Pro

  • Go to Aviasales and enter your departure city
  • Click "Flexible dates" or use the calendar view to see the cheapest travel days
  • Try the "Everywhere" search — it shows the lowest fares to any destination worldwide
  • Enable price alerts for your route so you get notified when prices drop
  • Sort results by price, duration, or best value to find your perfect option
  • Check nearby airports — flying into a secondary airport sometimes saves significantly

💡 Pro Tip

Book mid-week (Tuesday or Wednesday) for better deals. Prices on popular routes spike on Fridays and Sundays as leisure travelers search. The savings can be $30–$80 on a single ticket.

More Tips for Even Cheaper Flights in 2026

  • Set multiple price alerts for different date ranges on the same route
  • Be flexible with layovers — Aviasales often finds great multi-city options
  • Book 6–8 weeks ahead for international routes for the best balance of price and availability
  • Combine with eSIMs and tours from other deals on this site for full trip savings

Aviasales vs Other Flight Search Engines
