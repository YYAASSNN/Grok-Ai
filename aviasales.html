Why Aviasales is Great for Finding Cheap Flights

  • Compares 2000+ airlines and ticket offices instantly
  • "Everywhere" search mode — discover the cheapest destinations from your city

How to Use Aviasales Like a Pro (Step-by-Step)

  1. Go to Aviasales and enter your departure city
  2. Click "Flexible dates" or use the calendar to see the cheapest days
  3. Try the "Everywhere" search — it shows the lowest fares to any destination
  4. Enable price alerts for your route so you get notified when prices drop
  5. Sort results by price, duration, or best value
  6. Check nearby airports — sometimes flying into a secondary airport saves a lot

Pro Tips for Even Cheaper Flights in 2026

  • Book mid-week (Tuesday or Wednesday) for better deals
  • Set up multiple price alerts for different date ranges
  • Be flexible with layovers — Aviasales often finds great multi-city options
  • Combine with eSIMs and tours from other deals on this site for full trip savings
