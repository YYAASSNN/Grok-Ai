What is Lycamobile USA?

Lycamobile USA is part of the global Lycamobile network — one of the world's largest MVNOs (Mobile Virtual Network Operators). In the US, Lycamobile offers low-cost prepaid SIM plans that run on nationwide coverage networks, with generous international calling minutes included as standard.

It's particularly popular with international visitors, expats, and students who need affordable US mobile service with the ability to call home cheaply.
