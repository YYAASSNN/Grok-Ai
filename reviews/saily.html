What is Saily?

Saily is a travel eSIM service owned by Nord Security, the company behind NordVPN and NordPass. Its official site says it offers data plans in 200+ destinations and supports local, regional, and global travel use cases. Saily also stands out because it has introduced built-in security features such as ad blocking, web protection, and virtual location tools on supported plans.

At the same time, Saily's standard eSIM offering is still primarily a data product. According to Saily support, the service currently provides data-only eSIM plans, so it should not be treated as a normal voice-and-text SIM replacement.

Pricing & Plans

Saily positions itself as an affordable travel eSIM service, but prices still vary by country, region, and data size. Some destinations start low for small plans, while heavier-use plans cost more. As with other eSIM providers, the best comparison is destination by destination rather than assuming one provider is always cheapest everywhere.
