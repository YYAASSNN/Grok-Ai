What is Airalo?

Airalo is a travel eSIM marketplace that lets you buy mobile data plans for more than 200 countries and regions before or during a trip. Instead of visiting a SIM shop after landing, you install an eSIM on a compatible device and activate a plan digitally. Airalo offers local, regional, and global options, which makes it useful for both single-country trips and multi-stop journeys.

One important detail is that most Airalo plans are data-only. Some products, such as selected Discover+ plans, also include calls and texts, but you should always check the exact pack before you buy.

How Does Airalo Work?

Getting started with Airalo takes only a few minutes. You download the Airalo app or visit the website, search for your destination, and choose a plan that fits your data needs and trip length. After purchasing, you receive a QR code or can install the eSIM directly through the app.

To install, you go into your phone's mobile data settings, select "Add eSIM," and either scan the QR code or use the direct app installation. The process typically takes under five minutes and the eSIM is ready to activate when you land. There is no need to visit a local shop or swap a physical SIM card.

Once activated, you use the Airalo eSIM as your data connection. Your home SIM stays in the phone and can still receive calls and texts — the eSIM just handles the data in your destination country.

Pricing & Plans

Pricing depends on destination, data allowance, and validity period. Airalo offers a wide range of plan sizes, so it works well for light users who only need maps and messaging as well as travellers who need a larger data package for streaming or video calls.

As a general guide, short-trip local plans for popular destinations tend to start from a few dollars for 1GB with 7-day validity. Regional plans covering multiple countries cost more but offer better value for multi-country itineraries. Global Discover+ plans with calls and texts are priced at a premium but eliminate the need for a separate roaming plan.

Because prices vary by country, it is always better to search your specific destination on the Airalo app or website to get accurate current pricing before you travel.
