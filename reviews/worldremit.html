What is WorldRemit?

WorldRemit is an online money transfer platform that lets you send money abroad without visiting a physical branch. Founded in 2010, it has grown into one of the most widely used international transfer services, supporting transfers to 130+ countries across Africa, Asia, Europe, and the Americas.

Its key appeal is convenience — you can send money from your phone or computer in minutes, with the recipient receiving funds via bank deposit, cash pickup, mobile wallet, or airtime top-up depending on the destination.

Pricing & Fees

WorldRemit fees depend on the sending country, receiving country, delivery method, and transfer amount. In many corridors, fees are competitive compared to traditional banks. The service displays the exact fee and exchange rate before you confirm the transfer, so there are no hidden surprises at checkout.

As with all money transfer services, the cheapest option varies by corridor. The best approach is to compare the fee and exchange rate for your specific route before sending.
