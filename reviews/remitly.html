What is Remitly?

Remitly is an international money transfer service that lets senders move money abroad through its website and app. According to Remitly, it supports transfers to 175+ countries and territories. Depending on the destination, recipients may be able to receive money by bank deposit, cash pickup, mobile money, home delivery, debit card deposit, or similar local methods.

That broad delivery network is what makes Remitly attractive. Instead of offering one generic payout route everywhere, it adapts to how people in each country actually receive funds.

Key Features

  • ✓ Transfers to 175+ countries and territories
  • ✓ Multiple delivery methods by destination
  • ✓ Bank deposit, cash pickup, mobile money, and more
  • ✓ App and web transfer options
  • ✓ Clear transfer setup with destination-specific providers

Pricing & Plans

Remitly pricing depends on the send country, receive country, delivery method, transfer speed, and exchange rate. Because those variables change by route, it is more accurate to judge Remitly by the exact corridor you need than by a broad claim that it is always the cheapest option.
