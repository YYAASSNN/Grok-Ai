What is Hostinger?

Hostinger is a web hosting platform that offers shared hosting, cloud hosting, VPS hosting, domain registration, and website-building tools. Its official UK site positions it as a full platform for launching and growing a website, while its AI website builder pages focus on automatic site generation, drag-and-drop editing, and built-in AI tools for SEO, logos, images, and products.

That combination makes Hostinger appealing to beginners who want an all-in-one setup and to users who may later upgrade from a simple site to something more demanding.

Key Features

  • ✓ Shared, cloud, and VPS hosting options
  • ✓ AI website builder with drag-and-drop editing
  • ✓ Domain and hosting integration in one account
  • ✓ Marketing and site-management tools
  • ✓ 24/7 support and money-back guarantee on many plans

Pricing & Plans

Hostinger is generally positioned as a value-focused host, but pricing depends heavily on the product type and subscription length you choose. Introductory prices can look very low, while renewals are higher, so the most accurate way to judge cost is to check the renewal terms and contract length before buying.
