What is Yesim?

Yesim is a travel eSIM provider that advertises coverage in 200+ destinations. Its official site highlights prepaid and unlimited data plans, one-click installation, and app-based management. Unlike many eSIM brands, Yesim also markets extra services around the connectivity product, including a built-in VPN history and separate virtual number offerings.

The key point for accuracy is this: not every Yesim product is bundled into one standard eSIM plan. Data plans are the core product, while VPN and virtual-number features may be offered as separate or optional services depending on what you buy.
