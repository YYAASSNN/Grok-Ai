What is Aviasales?

Aviasales is one of the largest flight comparison engines in the world, particularly dominant in Eastern Europe and Asia. It searches across 700+ airlines and hundreds of booking sites simultaneously, showing you the cheapest available fares with no markup and no hidden booking fees.

Unlike some competitors, Aviasales simply displays fares and directs you to book directly with the airline or booking site — keeping costs as low as possible.

Key Features

  • ✓ 700+ airlines compared in real time
  • ✓ No booking markup — prices direct from source
  • ✓ Price calendar to find cheapest travel dates
  • ✓ Fare alerts sent by email when prices drop
  • ✓ Flexible search — explore cheapest destinations
  • ✓ Baggage filter to include/exclude luggage costs

Pricing & Plans

Aviasales is completely free to use. There are no booking fees charged by Aviasales — you pay exactly what the airline or booking site charges. The platform earns via affiliate commissions from travel partners, which never affect the prices you see.
