What is Aviasales?

Aviasales is a flight metasearch engine — not an airline and not a traditional online travel agency. Its job is to search fares, compare options across the web, and redirect you to the airline or ticket seller that will actually take payment and issue the ticket. According to Aviasales, it compares fares across 2,000+ airlines and ticket offices.

That makes Aviasales most useful for price comparison. It is a good fit if you want to scan many fare options quickly across airlines you may not have thought to check directly. The key thing to understand upfront is that your booking is made with whoever Aviasales sends you to — not with Aviasales itself.

How Does Aviasales Work?

Using Aviasales is straightforward. You enter your origin, destination, and travel dates on the website or app, and Aviasales pulls fares from its network of 2,000+ sources in real time. You can filter by price, stops, airline, departure time, and trip duration to narrow down the results.

When you find a fare you want, clicking it takes you directly to the airline's website or the third-party ticket agency selling that fare. You then complete the booking and payment on that external site. Aviasales is purely the comparison layer — it does not handle your booking, hold your payment, or issue your ticket.

This is an important distinction because it means your post-booking relationship for changes, cancellations, or refunds is with whoever issued the ticket — usually the airline or the agency you booked through.

Pricing & Plans

Aviasales does not charge a booking fee because it is a comparison platform, not a ticket seller. There are no subscriptions, memberships, or service charges from Aviasales itself. The total price you pay is set entirely by the airline or agency you are redirected to after clicking a result.

The value of Aviasales comes from the breadth of its comparison. By pulling fares from a large number of sources simultaneously, it increases the chance you will see a cheaper option than if you searched each airline directly. This is particularly useful for routes with multiple airline options or when you have flexible travel dates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Aviasales a booking site or a comparison site?

Aviasales is a flight metasearch platform. It compares fares across 2,000+ airlines and ticket offices, then redirects you to the airline or third-party agency for final booking. Aviasales itself does not issue tickets.

Is Aviasales legit and safe to use?

Yes, Aviasales is a legitimate and widely used flight comparison tool. It is transparent about being a metasearch engine and does not take payment itself — booking is completed directly with the airline or agency it redirects you to.

Is Aviasales useful for flexible dates?

Yes. Aviasales includes a price calendar that lets you compare fares across multiple dates to find cheaper options. Final pricing and availability depend on the site you book through.

Does Aviasales charge a booking fee?

Aviasales is free to use for searching and comparing flights. It does not charge a fee. Any charges are set by the airline or ticket agency you are redirected to after clicking a result.

How many airlines does Aviasales compare?

Aviasales compares fares across 2,000+ airlines and ticket offices simultaneously, making it one of the broader flight comparison tools available for travellers.

What happens if my flight is cancelled after booking through Aviasales?

Because Aviasales is a metasearch tool, your booking relationship is with the airline or agency that issued the ticket. For cancellations, refunds, or changes, contact that airline or agency directly — not Aviasales.
