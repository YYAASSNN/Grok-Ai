What is Aviasales?

Aviasales is a flight metasearch engine, not an airline and not a traditional online travel agency. Its job is to search fares, compare options, and then redirect you to the airline or ticket seller that will actually take payment and issue the ticket. According to Aviasales, it compares fares across 2,000+ airlines and ticket offices.

That makes Aviasales most useful for price comparison. It is a good fit if you want to scan many fare options quickly, but you still need to pay attention to which seller you are redirected to before you book.

Key Features

  • ✓ Metasearch model rather than direct ticket sales
  • ✓ Compares 2,000+ airlines and ticket offices
  • ✓ Search filters for price, timing, and route preferences
  • ✓ Helps surface cheaper and alternative flight options
  • ✓ Free to use for search

Pricing & Plans

Aviasales does not charge a booking fee for using its search engine because it is primarily a comparison platform. Final pricing depends on the airline or agency you choose after you click through. That means the total value of Aviasales comes from comparison convenience rather than any special membership or subscription plan.
