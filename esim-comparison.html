Yesim – Best for Unlimited Data

📱 Yesim eSIM Review

Yesim offers truly unlimited data plans in 150+ countries with no fair-use throttling on most packages. It's the go-to choice for travelers who stream, work remotely, or spend weeks abroad without wanting to think about data limits.

Pros

  • Truly unlimited — no data caps
  • Instant setup in under 60 seconds
  • Great for long stays (30+ days)
  • 10–20% off for new users

Cons

  • Pricier than Saily for unlimited
  • Slower speeds in very busy areas
  • 150 countries (not the widest)

Airalo – Best for Short Trips & Flexibility

📱 Airalo eSIM Review

Airalo was one of the first major eSIM providers and remains the most popular choice in 2026. Its pay-as-you-go model in 200+ countries is perfect when you only need data for a week and want to buy exactly what you need without a large upfront cost.

Pros

  • Widest coverage — 200+ countries
  • Easy top-ups anytime
  • Real-time usage tracking in app
  • Great for 7–30 day trips

Cons

  • No unlimited plans
  • Gets expensive for heavy users
  • Speeds slow after heavy usage

Saily – Best Price on Unlimited Plans

📱 Saily eSIM Review

Saily is the newest brand from the NordVPN team and has quickly gained a reputation for offering the cheapest unlimited data plans in many destinations. The built-in ad blocker and privacy protection is a unique bonus you won't find elsewhere.

Pros

  • Cheapest unlimited in many countries
  • Built-in ad blocker & protection
  • No speed throttling on unlimited
  • Backed by NordVPN's security

Cons

  • Newer brand, fewer user reviews
  • Not as wide as Airalo in remote areas
  • Support slower during peak seasons

Which eSIM Should You Choose?

  • Choose Yesim if you need unlimited data and will be traveling for 2+ weeks — especially for streaming, remote work, or heavy maps use.
  • Choose Airalo if your trip is 1–2 weeks, you just need occasional data, or you want the widest possible country coverage.
  • Choose Saily if you want the cheapest unlimited plan available and value extra privacy protection from NordVPN.
