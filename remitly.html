What is Remitly?

Remitly is one of the world's leading international money transfer services, designed to make sending money abroad as fast, affordable, and reliable as possible. With coverage in 170+ countries and support for bank transfers, mobile wallets, and cash pickup, Remitly serves millions of customers sending money home.

A strong focus on transparency means you always see the exact exchange rate and fee before confirming — no surprises.

Remitly charges a small transfer fee (often $0–$3.99) depending on the destination and delivery speed. Exchange rates are competitive and shown upfront.
