What is Klook?

Klook is the world's leading travel experiences platform, specialising in tours, activities, theme park tickets, transport passes and day trips. Founded in 2014 in Hong Kong, Klook now offers over 500,000 experiences across 100+ countries and has served over 50 million travellers globally.

Whether you're looking for skip-the-line museum tickets in Paris, a day tour in Tokyo, or a transfer from the airport in Bangkok, Klook has it — all bookable via mobile with instant confirmation.

Key Features

  • ✓ 500,000+ experiences across 100+ countries
  • ✓ Instant mobile vouchers — no printing needed
  • ✓ Best-price guarantee on most bookings
  • ✓ Flexible cancellation on eligible activities
  • ✓ Klook Credits cashback reward scheme
  • ✓ 24/7 customer support in multiple languages

Pricing & Plans

Klook is free to use — you only pay for the experiences you book. Prices vary by activity and destination, but Klook frequently offers discounts of 10–40% compared to booking directly on-site. A Klook Credits programme rewards repeat customers with cashback on future purchases.
